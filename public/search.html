I found a child

If you have found or seen a child you believe may be lost or taken, upload a clear photo. We will compare it against every registered missing child. If there is a strong match (80% or higher), the child's family is alerted.

On a phone you can take the photo right now. Otherwise choose a clear photo from your device.

A possible match is not proof. Please do not confront anyone. Share any strong match with your local police or child-protection authorities, who can verify it safely.
